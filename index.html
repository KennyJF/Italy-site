Welcome to Italy!

The first civilization in Italy was the Etruscan, which arose about 800 BC in central Italy.
In 510 BC Rome, which was being ruled by the Etruscans at the time, rebelled.
The last Etruscan city fell to the Romans in 265 BC.
Over time the national language of Italy has changed
but the two biggest languages spoken across the ages are
Classical Latin spoken by the Romans and Italian spoken by modern day Italy.
The population of todays Italy is roughly 59 million people.
